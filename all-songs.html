Tips for Minecraft

  • 1. Only craft one wooden tool (pickaxe), and get stone with it for other tools!
    Don't waste your wooden planks on spades, axes or swords!
  • .2 Never dig straight down as you can fall into a ravine or an underground lake of lava!
  • .3 Never dig straight up unless you have an iron or diamond shovel in your hotbar,
    since gravel and sand will suffocate you in a matter of seconds. That's why you need to be quick with the shovel!
  • .4 Found Diamond? Make sure it's not touching any lava before you mine it!
  • .5 Don't even try to kill that skeleton as newly spawned in a Hard / Hardcore world.
  • .6 Don't waste food! Only eat that cooked pork chop if there is 4 or more
    'Food Nuggets' missing instead of eating it when only 1!
  • .7 Always remember that when you are are playing minecraft, there are creepers. You might
    forget its existence sometime happily mining underground, and then you turn around and one is in your face and makes you jump out
    of your chair as well as giving you a minor heart attack.
  • .8 Be 100% sure the skeleton will shoot you before you can hit it? Block with your sword when it shoots!
  • .9 If the creeper is going to blow up, and there is no time to get away from it, look at it and block!
    Blocking against creeper explosions reduces a very high amount of explosive damage!
  • .10 If you are playing on hard / hardcore and don't want any zombies to break in, replace your door with
    2 fence gates on top of each other!
  • .11 Escaping a spider but cannot sprint? Walk backwards and punch at it, it's leaping
    attack is faster than your walking speed!
  • .12 Don't do risky jumps unless you are good at it (example a small lava lake or a ravine).
  • .13 1 spider, 1 creeper, 1 zombie and 1 skeleton all coming against you? Use your bow on the
    Creeper and the Skeleton to keep them away, and use your sword against the leaping spider and the zombie!
  • .14 Super scared of endermen for some reason? Wear a pumpkin, and put water around your house.
  • .15 Fighting an enderman? Hit it in the legs, this highly reduces the chance of it to teleport away
    or behind you, or be tactical and pour a bucket of water on him!
  • .16 Escaping an enderman? Stand in water!
  • .17 Horde of creepers coming for you? Snipe them or flee.
  • .18 Make sure that the water 'down there' is 2 blocks deep or else you will still take fall damage.
  • .19 Eat rotten flesh, it makes you very hungry, but it heals up 2 food nuggets and depletes about 1 food nugget unless you are sprinting. Which means that rotten flesh is useful.
  • .20 Keep in mind that Poison cannot remove your last half a heart (kill you), but what the danger is about
    it that it takes you down to so health that any mob that hits you or fall damage will kill you.
  • .21 Do you have no food on you but a spider eye, and your just about to not regenerate health and you
    need to regenerate it? Make sure there are no monsters near you, eat the
    spider eye. It will poison you for 5 seconds, but it will make you regenerate health again.
  • .22 Store your important items in an ender chest, creepers can not destroy it, and other players
    cannot steal from you as it keeps each players items that was put into it, also
    if you destroy it with items inside, you won't have access to those items until you open a new one.
  • .23 Only break an ender chest with a pickaxe with the enchantment Silk Touch, because if you do it
    with any pickaxe that hasn't Silk Touch on it, the chest will break into 8 pieces
    of obsidian and you have to get another eye of ender. The Silk Touch enchantment is only possible
    to get on pickaxes, what it does is to give you the exact block you mine. For example: Pickaxe on Stone: Gets
    Cobblestone. Pickaxe with Silk Touch on stone: Gets Stone, not Cobblestone. Same works with Ice, grass etc.
